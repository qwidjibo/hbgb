Almost fifty percent of our camp is made up up healers. We offer alternative healing modalities such as Shamanic and Spiritual Healing, Energy Therapies, Counseling, Yoga, Meditation, Tantra and whatever other talents show up at our camp each year. Just as with support positions, we expect all our healers to offer 4 hours per day of their time in our healing space.

Our healing space usually contains 12 - 14 heaing stations and some floor space to work on. Healers can also work out in the chill area, cycling among the guests and offering mini-sessions or other comforts.
